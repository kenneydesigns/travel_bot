=== OCONUS vs CONUS PCS Differences ===

CONUS (Continental U.S.):
- BAH provided
- TLE up to 5 days
- HHG moved via local TMO

OCONUS (Outside Continental U.S.):
- OHA + MIHA may apply
- TLA instead of TLE
- Passport and immunizations may be required
- More documentation required for dependent travel
