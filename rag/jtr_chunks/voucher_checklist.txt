=== Travel Voucher Submission Checklist ===

Required Documents:
- Orders (and any amendments)
- DD Form 1351-2 (if using hard copy)
- Receipts for all lodging
- Receipts for expenses over $75
- Rental car agreement and fuel receipts
- Weight tickets for PPM
- Itemized airfare/baggage receipts

Tips:
- Submit via DTS for TDY or eFinance for PCS
- Sign and date your voucher
- Keep copies of all documentation
