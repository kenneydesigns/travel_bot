=== Household Goods (HHG) Shipment ===

- The government will pay to move your HHG during PCS, within weight limits.
- Contact the TMO or DMO to schedule your shipment.
- You may ship from and to approved locations only.
- Excess weight or unauthorized storage may result in out-of-pocket costs.
- Save a copy of your weight ticket and shipment documents for your PCS voucher.
