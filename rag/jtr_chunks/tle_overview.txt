=== Temporary Lodging Expense (TLE) ===

- TLE reimburses lodging and meal costs incurred in the CONUS during PCS.
- You may claim up to 5 days at the losing or gaining installation.
- Must use commercial lodging or base lodging when available.
- Reimbursement is based on actual expenses, not a flat rate.
- Submit TLE claims via eFinance with itemized lodging receipts.
