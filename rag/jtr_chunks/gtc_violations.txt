=== Government Travel Card (GTC) Violations ===

Common GTC Misuses:
- Personal purchases unrelated to official travel
- Fuel for POV when not authorized
- Meals or transportation for dependents
- ATM cash advances without commander approval
- Using GTC outside the dates on orders

Consequences:
- Denial of reimbursement
- Disciplinary action (military or civilian)
- Possible card suspension or revocation
