=== Mileage Reimbursement ===

- Mileage is reimbursed for travel by POV (Privately Owned Vehicle) using DTMO rates.
- Rates vary by year and vehicle type.
- Reimbursement requires odometer readings or Google Maps printout.
- Only one member per vehicle may claim mileage.
- PCS mileage claimed via eFinance; TDY mileage via DTS.
