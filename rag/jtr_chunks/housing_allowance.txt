=== Housing Allowance Overview ===

- BAH (Basic Allowance for Housing) is provided for CONUS assignments and based on duty location, pay grade, and dependent status.
- OHA (Overseas Housing Allowance) is provided for OCONUS locations and reimburses actual rent and utilities, up to a cap.
- You cannot receive both BAH and OHA at the same time.
- BAH is taxable; OHA is not.
